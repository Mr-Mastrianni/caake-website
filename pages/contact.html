Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical process for starting a project with CAAKE?

We begin with a consultation to understand your business needs, then provide a proposal outlining our recommended approach, timeline, and costs. Once approved, we move into development, testing, and implementation phases.

How long does it take to implement an AI solution?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. Simple automations might take a few weeks, while comprehensive AI solutions could take several months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

Do you offer ongoing support after implementation?

Yes, we offer various support packages to ensure your AI solutions continue to perform optimally. These include maintenance, updates, and performance monitoring.

Can you integrate AI solutions with our existing systems?

Absolutely. We specialize in integrating our AI solutions with existing business systems and processes to ensure seamless operation and maximum value.

What industries do you serve with your AI solutions?

We work with clients across diverse industries including retail, healthcare, finance, manufacturing, logistics, and professional services. Our solutions are adaptable to specific industry needs and challenges.

How do you ensure data security and privacy with AI implementations?

We implement industry-standard security protocols and compliance measures for all our AI solutions. This includes data encryption, secure access controls, and adherence to regulations like GDPR and CCPA where applicable.
