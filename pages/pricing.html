Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know which plan is right for my business?

We offer a complimentary AI readiness assessment to help determine the best fit for your needs. Our experts will evaluate your business goals, current systems, and budget to recommend the optimal plan.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can easily upgrade your plan at any time as your needs grow. Downgrades can be processed at the end of your billing cycle.

What is your contract length?

Our Basic and Professional plans require a 6-month minimum commitment, while Enterprise plans typically have a 12-month term. We offer discounts for annual payments.

How do you measure performance for the value-based pricing?

We establish clear KPIs at the beginning of our engagement, tailored to your business objectives. These may include cost savings, efficiency improvements, revenue increases, or other measurable metrics.

Do you offer implementation support?

Yes, all our plans include implementation support to ensure a smooth onboarding process and successful adoption of our AI solutions.
